Meet the OT

Natalie is an AHPRA-registered Occupational Therapist with years of experience across a wide range of health settings, including in the home, schools, hospitals and the community. At the core of her approach is a commitment to helping her clients enhance their daily lives and achieve their broader life goals. She believes in a holistic, strengths-based approach, working alongside you to build confidence, independence, and practical skills that make everyday life easier and more meaningful.
