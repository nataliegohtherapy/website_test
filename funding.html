Funding & Service Pathways

National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) Services

Natalie Therapy accepts self-managed, plan-managed, and NDIA-managed participants. Support can include functional assessments, ongoing therapy, skill development, sensory and emotional regulation support, assistive technology, home modifications, and reports to help achieve your goals and increase independence.

Commonwealth Home Support Programme (CHSP) and Support at Home program

Natalie Therapy supports older adults to remain safe, independent, and confident at home. Services may include falls prevention, equipment prescription, home safety assessments, minor home modifications, mobility support, and strategies to assist with everyday activities and wellbeing.

Private Occupational Therapy Services

Private Occupational Therapy services are available for individuals and families seeking flexible, personalised support without the need for government funding. Services are tailored to your individual goals and may include functional assessments, ongoing therapy, sensory and emotional regulation support, equipment recommendations, home modifications, and strategies to support independence and participation in everyday life.
