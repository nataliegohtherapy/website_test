How I Can Help

My Approach

I believe that therapy is a collaborative process. My approach is rooted in empathy, respect, and the belief that you are the expert on your own life. I aim to provide a space where you feel safe to explore your thoughts and feelings without judgment.

We will work together to understand the root of your concerns and develop practical tools to help you move forward. Whether you are dealing with anxiety, stress, or relationship issues, my goal is to help you build the skills and confidence you need to lead a more fulfilling life.

I draw from a variety of evidence-based practices, tailoring my methods to meet your individual needs. Most importantly, I focus on the therapeutic relationship as the foundation for growth and healing.
