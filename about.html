About Natalie

Hi! I’m Natalie and I’m a passionate Occupational Therapist with over 8 years of experience supporting children, adults, and older adults across home, school, hospital, aged care, and community settings. I love helping people build confidence, independence, and engage in the activities that are most meaningful to them.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and person-centred, with a focus on creating practical and tailored strategies that support each individual’s goals. I enjoy working closely with families and support networks to help people feel empowered, capable, and valued in their everyday lives.
