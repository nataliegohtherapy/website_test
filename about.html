About Natalie

Hi! My name is Natalie and I’m a passionate Occupational Therapist dedicated to helping people build independence, confidence, and engage in the activities that they love!

With over 8 years of clinical experience, I have worked across a variety of settings including:

  • NDIS
  • Occupational rehabilitation
  • Hospital
  • Community
  • My Aged Care
  • Major and minor home modifications
  • Assistive Technology prescription and education
  • Splinting & hand therapy
  • Schools
  • Residential aged care
  • Paediatrics

Through these experiences, I have developed a broad range of skills supporting both children and adults with diverse needs and diagnoses. These include autism, ADHD, dementia, stroke, orthopaedic conditions, and trauma. I enjoy working with both children and adults, and empowering my clients to overcome barriers, develop new skills, and achieve meaningful outcomes, while creating supportive environments.

Looking forward to getting to know you and supporting you in working towards your therapy goals.
